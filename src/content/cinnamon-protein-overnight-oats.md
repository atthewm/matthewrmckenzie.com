# Cinnamon Protein Overnight Oats

A high-protein breakfast that makes itself while you sleep.

## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup rolled oats
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 0.5 tsp cinnamon
- 0.75 cup milk (any kind)
- 0.25 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
- Pinch of salt

## Toppings (optional)

- Sliced banana
- Walnuts or almonds
- Drizzle of almond butter

## Directions

1. Combine oats, protein powder, chia seeds, cinnamon, and salt in a jar.
2. Add milk, yogurt, and maple syrup. Stir well.
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight (at least 6 hours).
4. In the morning, stir and add toppings.
5. Eat cold or microwave for 1-2 minutes.

*Prep 5 jars on Sunday. Breakfast sorted for the week.*
