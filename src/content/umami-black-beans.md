# Umami Black Beans

A rich, savory pot of black beans with layers of umami flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb dried black beans, soaked overnight
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp miso paste
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 bay leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Olive oil

## Directions

1. Drain and rinse soaked beans.
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Saute onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic, cumin, and smoked paprika. Cook 1 minute.
4. Add beans, bay leaves, and enough water to cover by 2 inches.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cook 1.5-2 hours until tender.
6. Stir in soy sauce and miso paste. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Remove bay leaves and serve.

*Best served over rice with a squeeze of lime.*
