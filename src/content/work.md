## Principles

I hold a few things as non negotiable in how I work:

1. Thoughtful underwriting over speed. The best capital relationships are built on rigor, not urgency.
2. Alignment between capital partners and operators. Structures should reward patience and shared outcomes.
3. Clarity in communication. Investors deserve direct answers, especially when the news is mixed.
4. Durable business models. I focus on companies built for long term ownership, not quick exits.
