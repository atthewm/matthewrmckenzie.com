# Master City Loaf

A no-knead artisan bread with a crispy crust and soft, airy interior.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread flour
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp instant yeast
- 1.5 cups warm water
- Cornmeal for dusting

## Directions

1. Mix flour, salt, and yeast in a large bowl.
2. Add water and stir until a shaggy dough forms. Do not knead.
3. Cover with plastic wrap and let rest at room temperature 12-18 hours.
4. Turn dough onto a floured surface. Fold it over itself once or twice.
5. Shape into a ball and let rest on parchment, covered, for 2 hours.
6. Preheat oven to 450F with a Dutch oven inside for 30 minutes.
7. Place dough in hot Dutch oven. Cover and bake 30 minutes.
8. Remove lid, bake 15-20 more minutes until golden brown.
9. Cool on a wire rack before slicing.

*Patience is the secret ingredient.*
