# Brownies

Dense, fudgy brownies with a crackly top. No box mix needed.

## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 0.33 cup cocoa powder
- 0.5 cup all-purpose flour
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp baking powder

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease an 8x8 pan.
2. Melt butter in a saucepan. Remove from heat.
3. Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
4. Add cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Mix until just combined.
5. Pour into prepared pan and spread evenly.
6. Bake 20-25 minutes. A toothpick should come out with moist crumbs.
7. Cool completely before cutting.

*Underbake by 2 minutes for maximum fudginess.*
