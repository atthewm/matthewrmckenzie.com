## Ingredients
- 2 lb fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
- 1/2 cup raw honey (adjust to taste)
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- Optional: 1 tbsp chia seeds (for thickening)

## Steps
1. Wash, hull, and slice strawberries (halves or quarters).
2. Add strawberries and lemon juice to a saucepan over medium heat. Cook 10 to 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until berries break down and release juices.
3. Mash to your preferred texture (optional).
4. Stir in honey and simmer 10 to 15 minutes more, stirring frequently, until thickened.
5. Optional: stir in chia seeds and let sit off heat 10 to 15 minutes to thicken.
6. Cool, then transfer to sterilized jars.

## Storage
- Fridge: up to 3 weeks
- Freezer: longer storage
