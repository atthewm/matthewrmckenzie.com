## Yield
19 tortillas (42 to 45 g each)

## Ingredients

### Dry
- 150 g Masienda Heirloom Blue Corn Masa
- 145 g organic wheat flour
- 95 g vital wheat gluten
- 12 g oat fiber powder
- 9 g fine sea salt

### Wet
- 38 g grass-fed ghee, melted
- 390 g warm water (do not exceed)

## Instructions

### 1) Mix dry
1. Add all dry ingredients to a stand mixer bowl.
2. Mix on speed 1 for 30 seconds to evenly distribute.

### 2) Add wet
1. With mixer on low, add melted ghee, then warm water.
2. Mix until dough forms.

### 3) Knead
1. Use the dough hook.
2. Speed 1 to 2.
3. Knead 2 1/2 to 3 minutes only.
4. Stop as soon as dough looks cohesive and smooth.
5. Do not overknead.

### 4) Rest
1. Cover the bowl.
2. Rest 45 minutes at room temperature.

This rest is essential for:
- Masa hydration
- Gluten relaxation
- Fiber absorption

### 5) Portion
1. Divide into 19 equal pieces (42 to 45 g each).
2. Roll into smooth balls.
3. Rest 5 minutes before rolling.

### 6) Roll
1. Use parchment top and bottom.
2. Flatten by hand first.
3. Roll in passes, rotating 90 degrees each time.
4. Target diameter: 6.5 to 7 inches.
5. Slightly thicker than standard flour tortillas.
6. Do not dust dough directly with flour.

### 7) Cook
1. Preheat a dry cast iron or heavy skillet to medium-high.
2. Cook:
   - Side 1: 50 to 55 seconds
   - Side 2: 40 to 45 seconds
   - Optional third flip: 10 seconds
3. Look for:
   - Light brown spotting
   - Flexibility
   - No cracking
4. Stack immediately in a towel.
